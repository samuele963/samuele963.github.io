About the Museum

The Museum is an ongoing effort to document how electrical installations function and what they look like, from every country in the world, wherever possible.

What the museum covers:
MV-LV transformers (partially), LV distribution (partially), earthing, supplier's cutouts, power meters, breaker/fuse boxes, sockets, plugs, adaptors, power strips, appliance couplers (partially), industrial plugs and sockets (up to 480V)

What the museum does not cover:
HV and MV transformers, HV and MV power lines, any items >500V, DC supplies, household appliances, light fixtures
