Fujitsu Siemens Scenic T (model i815e)

Specifications

  • Processor: Pentium 3 @ 1GHz
  • RAM: 512MB of SDRAM
  • Graphics card: AGP
  • Operating System: Windows 98SE

About this computer

There's not much information on this model sadly, so I'll just describe my specific unit:
This model appears to have been built with business/workstation use in mind: the case is sturdy, it uses Torx thumbscrews to hold the covers closed and, most importantly, there is a "Designed for Windows 2000" sticker on it.
I've also seen other models in the Scenic T family of computers with smart card slots built in - again, reinforcing the idea that this was a business computer.
