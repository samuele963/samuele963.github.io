Eltos - a mysterious Italian video company

Note: this page is still unfinished

The fist ever video processor I got, which was more or less the spark that got me into experimenting with analog video, was the V3000 from a weird unknown company called Eltos. I now have three devices in total from them and, importantly, information.

Eltos was a video equipment manufacturer from Italy; while any specific information about them seem to have been lost to time, by what I can tell they seem to have been moderately popular here in Italy in the prosumer and lower end professional market, since I've been able to find a few of their devices on Ebay and other online shopping sites.

An overview

Eltos V2500 video effects

Eltos V3000 video signal conditioning

Eltos V4000 video switching unit

Other products from them

Their products seem to fall into two categories: on one hand there's the smaller stand-alone units like the ones I have. These seem to be the ones more easily found online and thus I imagine the most popular. On the other there's their rack-mount devices - these are much harder to find any information about.
